Who We Are

About Alpha

Alpha Martial Arts Gym is a premier combat sports institution under the umbrella of Global Sports Incorporated (GSI) - a network dedicated to developing world-class athletes and disciplined individuals through the art of martial combat.

We offer a wide spectrum of disciplines, from traditional Filipino arts like Arnis to the international sport of Muay Thai, tactical combat, knife fighting, boxing, and kickboxing. Every class is designed to strengthen the body, sharpen the mind, and build unshakeable character.

Our structured ranking system with belting ceremonies every 3 months ensures every student has clear milestones to achieve. Whether you're a beginner, a parent enrolling your child, or a competitor aiming for the podium - Alpha is where champions are forged.
